Our Services

SCC Specializes in sandblasting and paiting of structural steel and heavy equipment. Between the two owners of the company, there is over ten years of experience and expertise.

Tanks and pressure vessels

Sand Blasting services are used for the preperation of steel sufaces. SCC provides a full service shop as well as field services to meet your specific needs.

Fabtricated and Structural steel

All our staff are highly trained and have the capability to apply a 1 - 3 coat system on structural and fabricated steel.

Specialties

Tanks, pressure vessels
Internal and external
coating of pipes
Fabricated steel and steel structures
